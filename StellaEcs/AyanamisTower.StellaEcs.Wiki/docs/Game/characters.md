# Characters

## Memory

Characters have a sort of memory of past events and interactions.

The goal is to make something like this possible:

You deposed a character from their great empire -> The disappear only later to return with an armada.

"Do you trully think I would just give up? Leave it all behind? While you bath in my accomplishments? NO Today I reclaim what is mine, what you took from me without a grude, It was your greed that drove your ambitions now it will be your doom".

This is all part of the dynamic AI director and AI agents that characters are. Both should work together.
