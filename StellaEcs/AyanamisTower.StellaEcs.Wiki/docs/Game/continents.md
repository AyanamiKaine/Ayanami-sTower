# Continents

Continents are the smallest are of the game. They cannot be further subdivided and are part of celestial bodies like planets. A continent is host to a population and buildings where they work.

Populations can buy resources from directly from other continents on the same planet. Otherwise, goods first need to be imported to the planet.

# Capital Continent

Every planet has one capital continent where the spaceport, governor's seat, and main infrastructure live. Holding the capital means you control orbital access and planetary administration.

# War

Continents can be occupied and be effected by war.
